The Solo Mission

One Student. One Vision.

UniBolt wasn't built by a corporation. It was built by a student, for students.

To whoever is reading this:

My name is Harshit Kashyap. I am not a CEO in a suit. I am a 3rd-year B.Tech student at COER University, just like many of you. I attend lectures, I submit assignments, and yes, I stress about exams.

The Origin: Dorm Room, 2024

UniBolt didn't start in a fancy office in Bangalore. It started in my hostel room on a broken laptop. I saw a massive problem happening right in front of me: my friends were graduating with 8.5 CGPA but couldn't deploy a simple "Hello World" app to a live server. They knew the theory of normalization by heart but froze when asked to connect a MongoDB database.

I realized the education system was broken. It rewards memorization, not creation.

Why I Built This Alone

I didn't want to wait for the system to change. So, I decided to build the solution myself. I spent sleepless nights coding the dashboard, the leaderboard algorithm, and the certification engine. I learned React, Node.js, and Cloud deployment the hard way—by building this platform.

There is no massive team behind UniBolt. It's just me, writing code, fixing bugs, and answering your support emails between my own college classes.

The Core Philosophy

Why can we play video games for 6 hours straight but struggle to study for 30 minutes? Because games have instant feedback loops. I applied this psychology to coding.

UniBolt is a game where the objective is to build your career. The XP points are real skills. The leaderboard ranks are your market value.

Our Values

1. Builders First

We prioritize shipping code over memorizing theory. If you can't build it, you don't know it.

2. Radical Affordability

I kept the price at ₹200 (less than a pizza) because I believe elite education should not be gatekept by money.

3. No BS Certificate

Our certificates are verifiable via blockchain. No more fake paper certificates that mean nothing.
