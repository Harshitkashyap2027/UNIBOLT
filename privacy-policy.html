Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 01, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to UniBolt ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we process your data when you visit unibolt.in.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our educational services:

  • Personal Identity: Name, Email Address, Phone Number, University/College Name.
  • Technical Data: IP Address, Browser Type, and Device Information for security.
  • Performance Data: Code submissions, quiz scores, and leaderboard rankings.

3. Use of Your Information

We use your data solely for:

  • Generating unique, verifiable certificates.
  • Populating the public leaderboard (Usernames are abbreviated to protect privacy if requested).
  • Communication regarding daily tasks and platform updates.

4. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption and secure database protocols. We do not sell your personal data to third-party advertisers.

5. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, please contact us via the support section on your dashboard.
