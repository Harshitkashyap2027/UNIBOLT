Common Questions

Certificates are unlocked automatically once you reach Day 20 or Rank #10. Check the "Hall of Fame" page to download it.

Try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, ensure you are not using an ad-blocker that might block the compiler.
How do I change my avatar?
