The Algorithm for Success

I designed a 4-step protocol to take you from absolute beginner to job-ready developer.

1. Enlistment & Calibration

Register on the portal and select your specialization (Web Stack or AI). Your dashboard initializes a personal roadmap. We assess your current skill level to tailor the difficulty of initial tasks.

2. The Daily Grind

A new task unlocks daily at 09:00 AM.
• Brief: Read the documentation.
• Build: Write code in our online IDE.
• Submit: Push to GitHub.
Consistency builds your "Streak Multiplier" which boosts your XP.

3. Leaderboard Dominance

Earn XP for clean code and fast submissions. Compete on the leaderboard against students from other universities like IITs and NITs. Top 10 rankers of the week get premium badges.

4. Graduation & Verification

Complete 80% of tasks to mint your verifiable certificate. This certificate contains a QR code that employers can scan to see your exact project history and code quality scores.

Pro Tip: Don't just copy code. The system has an AI plagiarism detector. If you copy code from ChatGPT without understanding, you will get 0 XP for that day.
