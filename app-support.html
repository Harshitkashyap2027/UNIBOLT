App Dev FAQ

Flutter won't compile — what do I do?
Run flutter clean then flutter pub get. Ensure you have the latest Flutter SDK. Check the error message carefully — most build errors have clear descriptions.
Where is my Certificate?
Certificates are unlocked automatically once you complete Day 20 of your internship. Check the Hall of Fame page to download it.
How do I submit my app to the App Store?
Go to Hall of Fame > Submit Your App. Fill in the app name, description, screenshots, and link. Your submission will be reviewed by our team within 48 hours.
Android Studio not detecting device?
Enable Developer Options on your device, turn on USB Debugging, and ensure you have the correct USB drivers installed. Try running adb devices in terminal.
